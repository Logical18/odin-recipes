Banana Bread

Description

Banana Bread is a sweet, moist, and dense quick bread desserts . It is a versatile baking item. It is created with mashed bananas, flour, sugar, fats, and leveaned witb baking soda. This banana bread is really, really, yummy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and lightly grease 9x5inch loaf pan
  2. In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients until well mixed
  3. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan
  4. Put in preheated oven for ~ 60 minutes
  5. Take out bread to cool for ~ 10 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
