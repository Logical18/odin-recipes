New York Cheesecake

Description

New York cheesecake is a rich, dense, and creamy baked dessert with a buttery graham cracker crust. Its flavor has a slight tang and velvety texture. This Cheesecake is really, really, yummy.

Ingredients

  • 15 graham crackers, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and grease a 9-inch springform pan
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a medium bowl until well combined
  3. Press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust
  4. Mix the other ingridnets together in a bowl one at a time, until combined and smooth
  5. Pour filling onto prepared crust
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour
  7. After the 1 hour, turn the oven off, let the cheescake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5-6 hours to prevent cracking. Then chill in the refrigerator until serving
  8. Enjoy!
