Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is a sweet dessert pie made with a spiced pumpkin custard filling baked in a single pie crust. It is a popular dessert during the fall and winter holidays, specifically Thanksgiving, and it tastes really, really, yummy.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15-ounce) can pumkin puree
  • 1 (14-ounce) can Condensed Milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (9-inch) unbaked pie crust

Directions

  1. Gather all ingridents and preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Whisk pumkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt togeher in bowl until smooth
  3. Pour into crust. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes
  4. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F and continue baking for 35 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
